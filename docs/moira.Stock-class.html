Parameters:

  • ticker - The ticker symbol of the stock.
  • security_type - "ExchangeTradedFund" or "Stock"
  • current_price - Current price per share, rounded to the cent.
  • shares - Number of shares held.
  • purchase_type - "Buy" or "Short"
